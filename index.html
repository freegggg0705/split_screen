Split Screen URL Comparison Tool

Enter the URLs of the two webpages you want to compare (e.g., Chinese and English versions of a law or ordinance). The pages will open in new tabs or windows for side-by-side comparison.

Instructions for Split Screen Viewing

To view the two webpages side by side:

  • Click "Open in New Tabs" to open both URLs in separate tabs.
  • Manually arrange the tabs side by side on your screen (e.g., drag tabs to split your browser window or use your operating system's window management tools).
  • Alternatively, click "Open in Split Screen" to open both URLs in separate browser windows, which you can then arrange side by side.
